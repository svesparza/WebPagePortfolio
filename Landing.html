About:

I am a Software Engineer that wants to change the world for the better and improve people's lives.

One of my biggest projects that I am/have worked on is a face tracker for people with tremors. This would essentially help people with slight tremors to use their face to input keys or mouse inputs.

Not everyone can afford a fancy camera to use eye-tracking features. This will allow anyone with a cheap camera and decent lighting to enjoy their time on their computer without the hassle.
